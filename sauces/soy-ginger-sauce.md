# Soy Ginger Sauce
## Ingredients
* 150g soy sauce
* 200g brown sugar
* 14g butter
* 60g ginger
* 85g honey

## Directions
1. Peel ginger.
2. On medium high heat add soy sauce and sugar.
3. Finely grate in the ginger.
4. Bring to a boil.
5. Bring down heat so that the sauce simmers. Let the sauce thicken untill it is like syrup.
6. Turn off the heat. Add butter & honey.
